About me

I’m a Computer Science student at the University of Łódź, specializing in programming and algorithms. Currently, I work as an IT .NET Intern, where I develop and maintain business applications using C#, ASP.NET Core, Blazor, Entity Framework Core and SQL.

I enjoy building clean, reliable software — from backend logic and databases to interactive frontends. In my projects I focus on readable code, solid architecture, and practical problem-solving.

Alongside .NET, I work with C++ and Python on academic and personal projects (algorithms, simulations, data/ML experiments), and I’m always expanding my toolkit through hands-on work.
